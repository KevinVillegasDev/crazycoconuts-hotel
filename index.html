Our Story

More than a place to stay—
a place to belong

Welcome to our family. Nestled in the coastal town of San Antero, Córdoba, Crazy Coconuts is more than a destination—it's a home where every guest becomes family.

Our spacious, immaculately clean bed & breakfast offers the perfect sanctuary for those seeking authentic relaxation. Here, where tranquil beaches meet unspoiled countryside, you'll savor lovingly prepared homemade meals while surrounded by the safe, serene atmosphere where time slows down.

We believe in the simple things: fresh morning coffee on the terrace, afternoon naps in hammocks, and evenings filled with good conversation and even better food.

Explore the Area

Your adventure starts right outside our door

San Antero is full of hidden gems. We'll share our favorite spots and help you get there.

Most Popular

Island Hopping Tours

Hop on a local boat and explore pristine islands, secret beaches, and the famous bioluminescent waters.

Full day trip 20 min from here

Mangrove Forest Tours

Kayak through magical tunnels of mangroves. Keep your eyes open for monkeys and exotic birds!

Mud Volcano

Float in therapeutic volcanic mud - it's weird, wonderful, and your skin will thank you.

Rincón del Mar

Incredible snorkeling tours in crystal-clear water, with plenty of fresh seafood restaurants to refuel after.

Bird Watching

La Caimanera sanctuary is home to flamingos, herons, and hundreds of exotic species.

Local Village Markets

Explore Coveñas and Lorica. Fresh fruit, handmade crafts, and the friendliest people.
